About Us

Friends of MillHIll Missionaries

The Friends of Mill Hill is an organization of lay people at parish level with the aim of raising awareness of all Christians to support Mission work of the Church. Primarily, this is Spiritual support through prayers and sometimes where feasible materially through donations and mission tins. Its specific target is to support Mill Hill Missionaries from Kenya and Uganda who are working in various Countries over the world very often in challenging environments like in Pakistan, Cameroun, Uganda, Kenya, Malaysia, India and South Africa. In the true Spirit of our founder, Cardinal Herbert Vaughan, they are in situations of primary evangelization, at the forefront of the Church‚Äôs mission.

Objectives Of Friends of Mill Hill

  1. Prayers for all Mill Hill Missionaries working in all parts of the world.
  2. Support for Kenyans and Ugandans Mill Hill Missionaries , your own sons sent to spread the Word in other countries.
  3. Making Mill Hill Known.
  4. Prayer and support for Vocations.
  5. Finance- In support of Kenya/Uganda Mill Hill Missionaries.

Structure Of Friends of Mill Hill

  1. Voluntary Membership
  2. Leadership Structure: Chairperson, Secretary and treasurer.
  3. Mission Animation: Mission is ours, a sign of our faith maturity to share the precious gift of faith.
  4. Promotions: to be organized by the groups- tins, envelopes, mobile and other fundraising activities.
  5. Prayers: Monthly prayer sessions for the Missionaries, recollections and retreats, offering masses for missionaries, participating in the Novena to St. Joseph, annual Mission day.
  6. Other Activities: Meetings, parish involvements, distribution of magazines, attending Mill Hill functions like ordinations, distribution of magazines and tins, visiting Mill Hill formation houses or parishes.
  7. The group to be financial sustainable.
